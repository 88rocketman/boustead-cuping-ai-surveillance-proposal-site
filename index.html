Decision-ready operating sequence

One commercial loop. Every shift can run it.

A fixed chain of accountable actions, so security events become measurable outcomes.

Step 1

Detect

AI ingests camera and thermal inputs and flags threat candidates by severity and zone.

Step 2

Assign

Each incident is assigned to a named operator with required action and evidence source.

Step 3

Acknowledge

Operator confirms the incident in context before any closure is permitted.

Step 4

Resolve / Escalate

Choose patrol dispatch, supervisor review, drone verification, or false-positive result.

Step 5

Evidence close

Attach evidence references and timestamps and lock timeline entries.

Step 6

Audit and improve

Review median validation time, unresolved concentration by zone, and evidence quality.

Pilot blueprint

Every phase is gated for accountability, not just completion.

Safety baseline

SOP mapping, permissions, escalation contacts, and incident policy.

Week 3–6

Core adaptation

Auth, incident model, role model, and dashboard shell adaptation.

Week 6–9

Threat engine

Configure rules, inference contracts, duplicate suppression, and audit linkage.

Week 9–12

Hardware integration

Camera and thermal onboarding with telemetry and connectivity verification.

Week 12–15

Validation matrix

Scenario scripts for boar, intrusion, smoke/fire, and arson-risk pathways.

Week 15–18

Operations training

Operator drills, dispatch-to-close timing, and evidence review rhythm.

Week 18–21

Hardening
